Working the Case

Working the case is a 30-minute collaborative mystery game. Play the role of investigators as you uncover evidence, understand the suspects, and discover the culprit.

It is currently in draft form and you can play it online via Playingcards.io. For the game rules and instructions for setting up an online session, check out this Working the Case Guide.
