About Diegetic Games

Diegetic Games is Randy Lubin's game design studio. This site features story games meant to help players build rich worlds and narratives. Most of the games on this site are free to print and play; many are free to remix.

The games toward the bottom of the page are playable but still in development. Feel free to send feedback and play reports to randy@diegeticgames.com.

"Diegesis" is Greek for narrative or plot. In stories, something is diegetic if it exists within the fiction; diegetic music might come from a character's headphones. These games are all about creating compelling stories and immersive worlds - enjoy!

Premise: Setting, Character, Plot

Premise is a collaborative worldbuilding and storytelling game. There are three sub-games – Setting, Character, and Plot – and you can play them independently or chain them together to explore a world in depth. Each sub-game has it's own template and play consists of simultaneously filling out parts of the templates, passing the sheets, and building on each other's ideas.
