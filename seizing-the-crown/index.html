Seizing the Crown

Seizing the Crown is a collaborative storytelling game in which you create a political fantasy epic of rival factions vying for control of a kingdom. Tell the story of how they strive to consolidate power and eliminate rivals until only one remains.

This game is currently in late playtesting and you can play it online with live multiplayer. Check it out: Seizing the Crown Online
